How To Play

  1. Make sure MetaMask app is installed in your browser.
  2. Start by choosing the gaming mode you would like to play in.

    3. Higher risk = higher reward when you win!

    If you've never played before, it's highly recommended to try Free mode to see how it works and to make sure your minecraft is compatible with the server.

  3. Choose a character to help mold your gameplay style.

    4. Wanna just relax and watch the farm grow? Try out playing as a farmer.

    Really enjoy digging straight down into lava pits? Try playing as the miner and get a boost to your mining points.

    Enjoy making the opponent suffer by your hand? Be a warrior and boost your kill points.

  4. Once the MetaMask payment has been processed, you will be whitelisted and given IP address for the next match.

Points

At the start of every game, multiple items will be chosen at random. These items will be given a random point value.

If you hold in your character's inventory, chest, or ender chest any of the items that were randomly chosen at the beginning of the game, you will be awarded the points value of those items per item you hold.

Die? Someone finds your chest and takes the contents? You will lose those points.
